Lasagna

A classic italian dish, lasagna is known around the world for being a treat. My personal memories of lasagna are of the frozen variety. On vacation, lasagna was an easy dish to serve after a day at the beach.

I would recommend having this dish with some garlic bread, a nice glass of wine, and maybe some parmesan cheese on the side in order to cheese it up a bit more. I personally would also recommend waiting a bit before diving in as the lasagna can store a lot of heat internally.

  • lasagna pasta
  • rao's pasta sauce
  • italian sausage
  • sun dried tomatos
  • ricotta cheese
  1. Heat over to 365 degrees
  2. Place one layer of pasta in the bottom of a lasagna dish
  3. place a layer of sausage and a layer of cheese on top of the first layer of pasta. Add sauce as well
  4. Place another layer of pasta on top of the meat, cheese, and sauce
  5. add a thin layer of sauce to the pasta and then cover with ricotta cheese
  6. place the pan in the oven with aluminum foil covering the pan
  7. bake for 1 hour and thirty minutes
  8. take the foil off and bake for another 30 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
