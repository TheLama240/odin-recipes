Gazpacho

Gazpacho is a traditional spanish dish which is unique to spanish culture. It is often described as a cold tomato soup which doesn't do it justice. It is a tomato dish that is refreshing and salty. Perfect for a summer day!

The history of gazpacho comes from the spanish countryside. Summers in spain can be extremely hot so a cool tomato beverage was a respite for workers coming back from the field for a traditional siesta.

  • tomatos
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • vinegar
  1. cut the tomatos into quarters
  2. place the tomatos,salt, olive oil, and vinegar into the blender
  3. blend until you have a liquid
  4. place in the fridge and allow to cool for 30 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
